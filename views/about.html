About CampusConnect

Connecting students with meaningful campus experiences.

CampusConnect brings university events, workshops, club activities, networking opportunities, and academic sessions together in one convenient place.

Our purpose

Campus opportunities should be easy to discover.

Students often miss valuable campus events because information is spread across emails, posters, social media, course websites, and group chats.

CampusConnect provides one central platform where students can discover events, view event details, register, and organize their upcoming activities.

01

One central platform

Students no longer need to search through several different communication channels to find campus opportunities.

Who we support

Designed for students and organizers

CampusConnect supports both students looking for opportunities and organizers managing campus events.

01

For Students

Students can browse events, search by category, view complete event information, register, cancel registrations, and track upcoming activities.

  • Discover campus events
  • Register for available activities
  • Manage personal registrations
  • View upcoming events
02

For Organizers

Organizers can create and manage events, monitor registrations, track capacity, record attendance, and review participation statistics.

  • Create and update events
  • Monitor registration numbers
  • Track event capacity
  • Review attendance information
